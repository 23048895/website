About Us

PayLo was founded in 2024 with a clear mission: to empower small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in Singapore. Recognizing the challenges that many SMEs face in managing their financial transactions and maintaining transparency, we set out to create a platform that offers simplicity, reliability, and complete transparency. Our team is dedicated to providing innovative financial solutions that make it easier for businesses to thrive. With PayLo, SMEs can focus on their core operations while we handle their financial needs efficiently and securely. We believe in supporting local businesses, fostering growth, and contributing to the economic development of Singapore. Join us on our journey as we continue to innovate and provide top-notch financial services tailored for SMEs.
